The Mission of the Patriotic People

PFC is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping disabled veteran's. We take our mission here very seriously and dedicate our time, money and effort to helping Veterans in day to day situations.

At PFC we take pride in the veterans that we help on a day to day basis. We have helped over 100,000 veterans and we will continue to do so. With your donated help, time, and money we can continue to reach out to homeless veterans, helping with food, shelter, clothes, health care and more!

Thank you for your continued support. We, and the veterans that are helped by your donations are more than thankful.
